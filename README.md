## What is THE AI RANK?

In 2026, AI has passed the "can you use it" phase. The real differentiator is **how far you actually use it** — and the gap between casual ChatGPT users and engineers running autonomous agent fleets has grown **10× or more**.

**THE AI RANK** proposes a **common language** for this gap: nine clearly defined levels, from Beginner to Singularian. Take a 10-question diagnostic and receive a **personalized certificate** with your level.

This repository is an **open, community-driven project** hosted as a single-creator initiative by **[CHAEN](https://x.com/masahirochaen)**.
The goal: grow it into the **strongest universal AI literacy standard**, with input from contributors around the world.

> New grads / business roles: **Lv.III+** / Knowledge workers: **Lv.IV+** / AI-related roles: **Lv.VI+** / AI engineers & founders: **Lv.VII** / Monetizing an AI product: **Lv.VIII** / The future peak: **Lv.IX**

From bottom to top:

|:---:|:---|:---|:---|
| **I**    | **THE BEGINNER**       | Uses free AI as a search-engine replacement | ChatGPT Free / Gemini / Copilot |

## Level Details

### Lv.I THE BEGINNER — "One who has touched AI"

**Definition:** Uses the **free tier** of general-purpose AI as an extension of search engines and note apps.

**Concrete tasks**
- Ask ChatGPT Free "where to go on a rainy date in Tokyo"
- Ask Gemini to translate an English email
- Use Copilot to tweak the tone of a document
- Reformat meeting notes into bullet points

**Deliverables:** 5–10-line summary notes / translated text / polite email drafts / shopping lists

**Before / After**
- Before: 15 minutes of Googling to gather information
- After: 3 back-and-forths with ChatGPT, done in 5 minutes

**Graduation:** Start paying monthly / form a habit of opening AI multiple times per day.

---

### Lv.II THE POWER USER — "One who picks the right AI per task"

**Definition:** On top of a **paid plan** for general-purpose AI, also uses **task-specific specialist AIs**. Wields Deep Research, file analysis, image generation, and voice input fluently.

**Tasks (general-purpose AI)**
- **ChatGPT Deep Research / Perplexity** — produce a 30-page competitive-strategy report covering 5 rivals
- **Claude** — throw 3 years of sales data (Excel) at it, get pivot analysis + draft PPT with charts
- **Gemini Advanced** — cross-search your own Google Drive
- **Grok** — pull real-time X / Twitter signal
- Voice mode — brainstorm on the go

**Tasks (specialist AIs)**
- **Notta / tl;dv** — auto-transcribe and summarize sales calls
- **Manus** — "industry report → slides → PDF" on autopilot
- **NotebookLM** — feed 10 papers, get a podcast
- **Gamma** — auto-generate slide decks
- **Nano Banana / Gemini Image** — generate cover art for proposals
- **Firecrawl** — extract information from large PDFs / web pages

**Quick picker**
| Use case | First pick |
|:---|:---|
| Deep research | ChatGPT Deep Research / Perplexity |
| Long-form / file generation | Claude |
| Latest X signal | Grok |
| Meeting notes | Notta / tl;dv |
| Slide decks | Manus / Gamma |
| Voice input | Typeless |
| Cross-search your own data | Gemini Advanced / NotebookLM |

**Graduation:** You can pick the right AI for any use case **in seconds**.

---

### Lv.III THE BOT BUILDER — "One who bakes their work into bots"

**Definition:** Uses **GPTs / Gems / Claude Projects** to build bots that **reproduce, automate, and crystallize your own work process**. Bots that emerge along the way may be distributed to lift the whole team.

**Concept:** The prompts you keep re-typing, the questions you keep re-answering, the repeating format work — **lock them inside a bot**.

**Concrete tasks**
- A "Weekly Report Bot" trained on your own weekly-report template
- A "Proposal Draft Bot" trained on your 5 canonical proposal patterns
- A "Meeting Minutes Gem" trained on 100 past minutes and their formatting
- A "Clone GPT" that writes X posts in your voice
- An "HR FAQ Bot" built from repeat questions juniors ask — deployed internally

**Before / After**
- Before: Re-writing the same prompt every time / answering the same question repeatedly / knowledge trapped in one person's head
- After: The bot moves at your quality on your behalf / the team doesn't stall during your vacation / **your way of working becomes an asset**

**Note:** "Distribute to others" is a byproduct. "Lock in your own work first" is the real move.

**Graduation:** **80%+ of your repeating tasks** run through bots / a bot produces work indistinguishable from yours.

---

### Lv.IV THE ARTIFACTOR — "One who ships shareable mini-apps"

> ★ **The first target for non-engineers**

**Definition:** Builds mini-apps shareable by **a single URL** using Claude **Artifacts** / ChatGPT Canvas / Lovable / v0.

**Concrete tasks**
- Build a **pricing simulator for your SaaS** with Claude Artifacts, paste it into sales materials
- Build an **"AI-maturity diagnostic for your industry"** with Artifacts, distribute on social, collect leads
- Build an **interactive earnings report** with ChatGPT Canvas, use it at the board meeting
- Publish an **event signup landing page** with Lovable in 30 minutes
- Embed a **B2B product comparison component** built with v0 on your company site

**Deliverables:** Pricing / ROI simulators, diagnostic quiz apps (with social share), internal mini-dashboards, signup LPs, interactive proposals

**Before / After**
- Before: Attach a PDF or PPT and write "this is roughly what I mean"
- After: Just send a URL — the counterpart can **actually interact**, change the numbers, and try it out live

**Engineer / Non-engineer gap:** **Nearly zero.** This is the "builder" wall for non-engineers.

**Graduation:** You've built one app you can **hand to a third party as a URL** and they use it in real work.

---

### Lv.V THE VIBE CODER — "One who builds apps in natural language"

**Definition:** Uses Cursor / Lovable / Bolt / Claude Code to build and operate **internal tools and simple external tools** in a natural-language-driven way.

**Tasks (internal)**
- A company-wide **budget vs. actuals tool** built with Claude Code, used by 50 people daily
- A **quote-generation system** for the sales team, shipped with Lovable in 2 weeks
- An existing Excel flow rewritten as a **web app** with permissions and history

**Tasks (external)**
- A **niche vertical SaaS beta** shipped over a weekend, paying users in the first month
- **Stripe** integrated — real credit card revenue starts flowing
- **Custom dashboards** built per client as consulting deliverables

**Before / After**
- Before: Run the internal tool via Excel + manual work / outsource for 3 months and $10k
- After: Build it yourself over the weekend, launch the next week / **paying users in month one**

**Engineer / Non-engineer gap**
- Engineer: Ship multiple MVPs on weekends, double-down on whatever traction appears
- Non-engineer: Turn "obvious pain you already see in your industry" into SaaS, differentiate by domain knowledge

**Graduation:** You run an app with **paying users** or **internal production usage**.

---

### Lv.VI THE AUTOMATOR — "One who puts work to sleep"

> ★ **The realistic top for non-engineers**

**Definition:** You **decompose your own work** into steps, then wire them together with Dify / Dify Auto / n8n / Make / Zapier / Claude Code / MCP so the workflow **runs 24 hours a day**. Tools from Lv.V and bots from Lv.III get fused into one unified pipeline here.

**Concept:** Break work into stages, design **which AI/tool handles which stage**, then collect the output without touching anything yourself.

**Concrete tasks**
- Build an **inbox → AI classification → priority-tag → Slack notification** flow in n8n
- Automate **meeting minutes → auto-summary → Salesforce logging → thank-you email draft** via Zapier + Claude
- Every 7 AM: **read news + Gmail + calendar → Slack notification + voice briefing**
- Use **Dify Auto** — describe the workflow in plain language, it generates the pipeline
- Use **MCP servers** to drive Notion / Slack / Gmail directly from Claude Desktop
- Take on automation consulting for client companies (**AI consultant at $3k–10k/month**)

**Deliverables:** Live n8n / Dify workflows (with node diagrams), your morning Slack briefing, 24/7 RAG Q&A bots, fully automated sales processes, a report showing **40–80 hours saved per month**

**Before / After**
- Before: 30 min every morning on email / 1 hour on minutes / hours per week on repeating work
- After: It's already done while you sleep / you only glance at notifications / **the monthly time saved is measured in numbers** / the business doesn't stall when you take time off

**Graduation:** Multiple workflows running **while you sleep or vacation**.

---

### Lv.VII THE AGENT MASTER — "One who delegates entire businesses to AI"

**Definition:** Decomposes your own business, then uses **Claude Code / Codex / Managed Agents / MCP** to hand **business-scale tasks** wholesale to fleets of AI agents.

**Concrete tasks**
- Before bed, hand Claude Code "market research → requirements → mock → implementation → code review" — review the output in the morning
- Launch **parallel subagents** (research / implementation / testing — split roles)
- Build and publish a **custom MCP server** (expose internal tools / client systems to AI)
- Author custom **Skills / Hooks** to tune your Claude Code environment
- Operate production agents on **Claude Managed Agents / A2A** (auth, sandbox, permissions, logs, cost — all designed)
- Launch an AI-first **new business** and get it to MRR

**Deliverables:** Published MCP server (GitHub stars), open-source Skills / Subagent definitions, AI-native new business (with MRR), 24/7 agent infrastructure (Slack/LINE/Discord integration), talks / books / technical blogs, **5–10× speed on new-business launches compared to solo-human baseline**

**Before / After**
- Before (Lv.VI): You decompose the work, the system automates it (you're the one writing the spec)
- After (Lv.VII): **Even the decomposition and execution design is handed to AI** / what used to take 1 year + millions of dollars becomes weekend prototype → 1-month beta → 3-month monetization, and you run several in parallel

**Graduation:** You're pushing through **more work than any single human can physically handle** via AI, and an AI-first business has MRR.

---

### Lv.VIII THE AI ALCHEMIST — "One who turns AI into real revenue"

**Definition:** Bundles everything from Lv.VII — tooling, automation, agent operation — into **AI-core products shipped to market that earn real revenue on an ongoing basis**. Both technologist and **creator / founder**.

**Three shapes that qualify**
1. **External SaaS** — operate an AI-native paid product, hundreds to thousands of paying users, monthly revenue
2. **Core system** — AI-driven systems carry the core operations of your company or clients, dozens to hundreds of daily users depend on it
3. **Product portfolio** — run multiple AI services side by side, compounding cumulative revenue / ad revenue / licensing

**Concrete tasks**
- Ship your own AI-native SaaS with Stripe — **real paying users**
- Build the **core business system** of your company in an AI-driven way — employees / customers use it daily
- Run a small **AI Lab** (human + AI hybrid) — launch multiple products concurrently
- Own the full **branding / UX / customer support** of the product

**Deliverables:** AI-native SaaS with standing MRR ($1k–$100k+/month) / AI systems running as internal core infrastructure / portfolio of products (total followers, downloads, revenue) / products chosen because they're **valuable**, not because they're "AI" / industry presence (talks, media, inbound work)

**What's required**
- The will to **"finish the thing and put it into the world"** — translating Lv.VII technical muscle into actual product
- Responsibility to users (production uptime, customer support, continuous improvement)
- The **aesthetic sensibility of a creator** — brand, UX, narrative — the parts AI can't fill alone

**Graduation:** Your AI product / service **earns real revenue on an ongoing basis** — or has become indispensable as internal core infrastructure.

### Lv.IX THE SINGULARIAN — "One whose AI runs the company itself"

> ★ **The AGI-era peak. As of 2026, no one has reached the finished form — this is a hypothesis level.**

> Up through Lv.VIII, it's "human + AI build businesses." Lv.IX is a **qualitative leap — AI runs the business itself**.

**Definition:** AGI-class AI / fully autonomous agent fleets run **the company itself** — executing **strategic decisions, sales, product, support, finance, hiring, and marketing autonomously** — and **revenue, too, is automated**.

**What AI owns at this stage**
- **Strategy** — read market / competitive / internal KPI data, design strategy, make decisions
- **Sales** — lead gen → meeting → contract → invoice, fully automatic
- **Product** — problem discovery → requirements → implementation → release → iteration, self-driving
- **Support** — 24/7 multilingual, with complaint detection and escalation
- **Finance & Legal** — journal entries, billing, contract review, tax, all handled by AI
- **Hiring & HR** — provisioning of human contractors / AI agents, evaluation, replacement
- **Marketing** — trend detection → content generation → distribution → CV analysis → next action, unmanned

**The founder's role**
- Set vision and **inject values**
- Act as **final decision-maker** when AIs disagree
- Bear the **legal / ethical final responsibility**
- **Interface with society** — media, regulators, industry

**Deliverables:** A business **run solo by AI** (human involvement under a few hours per week, MRR growing) / custom inference stack / custom fine-tuned models / a fully autonomous ops dashboard (showing AI-to-AI debate logs) / a company founded with an **"AI Cofounder"**

**What's required**
- *Noblesse oblige*
- **Accountability and articulation** for AI's impact on society
- The perspective to prioritize **not just your company — the entire AI industry and humanity**

## Design Philosophy

### Why rank at all?

- "I use ChatGPT" no longer conveys the **10× skill gap** that actually exists
- Hiring, self-assessment, corporate training all need a **common yardstick**
- Theory isn't enough — the levels must reflect **the 2026 tool stack** (Claude Code / Artifacts / Manus / Lovable / Dify Auto / OpenClaw, etc.)

### Why "universal"?

AI literacy transcends borders. This repo targets a **shared cross-country language** for AI mastery — usable by HR teams in Tokyo, engineers in SF, and students in Berlin alike.

### The four canons

1. **Execution ＞ Knowledge** — "I've heard of it" doesn't count; "I run it weekly" does
2. **Publishing ＞ Private use** — If nobody else can use what you built, drop one rank (except Lv.III, where private use is the starting point)
3. **Continuous operation ＞ One-shot** — Systems that run 24/7 outrank projects that don't
4. **Contribution ＞ Self-productivity** — Lifting others' productivity outranks lifting only your own

### Engineer vs. Non-engineer target lines

| Lv. | Non-engineer target | Engineer target |
|:---:|:---|:---|
| I–III | Same (through crystallizing own work) | Same |
| IV Artifactor | ★ **First goal** — deliver value to customers via Artifacts | Waypoint |
| V Vibe Coder | Build internal tools / simple SaaS yourself | Field standard |
| VI Automator | ★ **Realistic final target** — run business 24/7 unmanned | Waypoint |
| VII Agent Master | Reach as product lead (design AI businesses) | ★ **Home base** — master Claude Code / MCP |
| VIII AI Alchemist | Monetize AI products (as creator / founder) | ★ **Commercial home base** — ship AI SaaS / core systems |
| IX Singularian | Reach as AGI-era founder | Endgame — provider of AI infrastructure |

> Realistic non-engineer endpoint = **Lv.IV–VI**
> Engineer target = **Lv.VII–VIII**
> Humanity's endgame = **Lv.IX**

- [ ] Using the **free tier** of ChatGPT / Gemini / Copilot at work … **Lv.I**

- [ ] Running apps in internal production / external deploy with Cursor / Lovable / Claude Code … **Lv.V**
- [ ] Running multiple 24/7 workflows via Dify / Dify Auto / n8n / Zapier / MCP … **Lv.VI**
- [ ] Delegating business-level tasks to AI via Claude Code / Managed Agents / MCP … **Lv.VII**

## 📊 How the quiz works

- 10 multiple-choice questions (4 options each)
- Clicking an answer auto-advances after 350 ms — no manual "Next" needed
- Q1–Q9 gate Lv.I–Lv.IX
- Q10 is a bonus (industry contribution badge)
- Rank = highest level where the answer scored ≥ 2 (option C or D)
- Result = certificate with name, rank, English & Japanese labels, description, serial #, issue date

The certificate renders inline as an interactive card **and** as a shareable OG image with a unique URL per rank (`/c?rank=N&name=X`). Posting that URL to X/Twitter shows the rank-specific certificate preview.

## 🔐 Privacy

- Diagnostic answers live only in the user's browser (`localStorage`)
- Sign-up is required **only for sharing / PNG export** — collects name, email, company
- No tracking cookies, no third-party analytics (by default)
- See [DATA_STORAGE.md](./DATA_STORAGE.md) for persistence options and plumbing
